Who am I?

My journey in tech started with a curiosity about how systems work and how they can be protected. This curiosity evolved into a strong passion for networking, system security, and software development, leading me to explore tools, techniques, and programming languages like Python and C.

Outside of academics, I love exploring Linux, working on cybersecurity projects, solving real-world problems, and staying updated on emerging threats and technologies in the security landscape.
